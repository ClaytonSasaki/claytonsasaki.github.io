Research

Simulating Upscale Growth Environments

U.S.-based studies have given us a good idea of the general environments which favor upscale growth of convective storms, including strong large-scale forcing and abundent low-level moisture. However, there is still a lot to learn about the local environment and how differences in this small scale environment affects convective organization. Central Argentina is a global hotspot for deep convection that rapidly organizes into larger convective systems, but the observational network in this region is limited. Therefore, high resolution convective permitting models are a useful tool for understanding inital upscale growth environments in this region.
